# **metaCAMPUS — Intellectual Property & Disclosure Carve-Outs**

## **1\. Pre-Existing Intellectual Property Carve-Out**

“Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to assign, transfer, or grant to metaCAMPUS any rights to intellectual property, inventions, algorithms, smart contracts, blockchain logic, decentralized identity frameworks, transcripts/SLO schemas, codebases, datasets, models, or other materials that (a) were conceived, created, or reduced to practice by me prior to my relationship with metaCAMPUS; or (b) were developed independently on my own time without the use of metaCAMPUS equipment, infrastructure, Confidential Information, or DAO resources (collectively, **“Pre-Existing IP”**).  
 I shall retain all right, title, and interest in such Pre-Existing IP. Upon reasonable request, I will provide a high-level, non-confidential description of any Pre-Existing IP that I intend to integrate or interoperate with the metaCAMPUS platform.”

---

## **2\. Residual Knowledge Carve-Out**

“metaCAMPUS acknowledges and agrees that nothing in this Agreement restricts my ability to use **general knowledge, skills, concepts, AI/ML expertise, protocols, architectural patterns, or techniques** that I naturally retain in my unaided memory (**“Residual Knowledge”**), even if acquired while contributing to the metaCAMPUS ecosystem, provided that such use does not result in the unauthorized disclosure of metaCAMPUS Confidential Information.”

---

## **3\. Existing Ventures, DAOs, Open-Source & Side Projects**

“Notwithstanding any IP assignment, confidentiality, or contribution requirements herein, metaCAMPUS acknowledges that I may continue to own, develop, operate, or contribute to **existing personal business ventures, DAOs, open-source projects, blockchain tooling, AI agents, research, or creative works**, provided that such activities (a) do not materially interfere with my obligations to metaCAMPUS, and (b) do not use or disclose metaCAMPUS trade secrets or Confidential Information.”

---

## **4\. Non-Confidential Information Carve-Out**

“The confidentiality obligations in this Agreement shall not apply to any information that:  
 (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement;  
 (b) was lawfully in my possession without restriction prior to disclosure by metaCAMPUS;  
 (c) is rightfully received from a third party without a duty of confidentiality; or  
 (d) is independently developed by me without reference to metaCAMPUS Confidential Information.”

---

## **5\. Legally Required Disclosure**

“Nothing herein prevents me from disclosing information when required by law, regulation, subpoena, or court order. To the extent permitted, I will promptly notify metaCAMPUS so that it may seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy.”

---

## **6\. Passive Investments & Financial Interests**

“Nothing in this Agreement shall restrict me from holding **passive investments** in other technology companies, blockchain protocols, education platforms, DAOs, or digital assets—including but not limited to major cryptocurrencies, Algorand-based tokens, or equity positions—provided that such investments do not involve the misuse or disclosure of metaCAMPUS Confidential Information.”

---

## **7\. Contribution Independence for DAO Governance**

“metaCAMPUS acknowledges that participation in **DAO governance**, including voting, staking, research, public commenting, or contributions to decentralized standards bodies, shall not be deemed a conflict of interest or an IP assignment event so long as such participation does not incorporate or reveal metaCAMPUS Confidential Information.”

---

## **8\. Academic & Industry Collaboration Carve-Out**

“I may continue to engage in **research, academic collaborations, industry working groups, standards bodies, conferences, publications, and open technical discussions** related to blockchain, digital identity, transcripts/SLO frameworks, or AI, provided that such activities do not disclose metaCAMPUS trade secrets.”
